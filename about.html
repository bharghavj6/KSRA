About Us

Welcome To KSRA & Associates

  • The firm is registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India on 22.02.2019 under the Chartered Accountants Act, 1949.
  • The firm is managed by four Members as Partners. The following are the Partners. 1. Mr. Munaga Ashok, FCA, 2. Mr. Shailesh Reddy Nalla, FCA, 3. Mr. Balasai Krishna Jakkampudi, FCA, 4. Mr. Raju Peddireddi, FCA.
  • The firm has been rendering consultancy services on the issues mainly relating to Taxation, Project Finance, Management Consultancy Services, Assurance and Certification Services, Accounting and Secretarial Works for various corporates. The firm is expertise in providing of GST services. Further, the firm is undertaking various GST related works such GST monthly returns, GST refunds, filing of annual returns and handling of department audits etc.
  • Our clientele comprises companies from various industries such as Pharmaceutical, Works contractors, Manufacturing units and Trading units.
  • In addition, the firm handles various internal audits of companies.
  • The firm is also involved in preparation of Project Reports for various Companies engaged in setting up Undertakings and 100% Subsidiaries/ Joint Ventures in the States of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, has successfully syndicated in arranging Short / Medium- and Long-Term finances towards project funding and working capital need from various banks.
